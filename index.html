KCCF PARTNERSHIP WITH NPBC

Kiambu County Clergy Forum (KCCF) has established a partnership with Nairobi Pentecostal Bible College (NPBC) in order to equip the clergy in Kiambu County with relevant knowledge and skills in Biblical Studies, Leadership and Church Ministries. This unique opportunity seeks to equip pastors who did not get full benefit of both primary and secondary education and yet are called by God to serve His people in various churches and ministries.
