ABOUT THE TRAINING

The Training Program is designed for helping the Clergy and Community Leadership to achieve the following qualifications:

Certificate

-Certificate in Biblical Counselling
-Certificate in Enterpreneurship
-Certificate in Biblical Studies and Leadership.
- Certificate in Community Development,
- Leadership and Church Ministries


N/B Certificate Program takes 4 months-1 day a week from 9:00am-4:00pm. Each student is required to pay Ksh. 1400/ per day as well as Ksh.2,000/= for graduation at the end of the training The charges include lunch and tea as well as teaching and notes. A resource centre and library is available for more studies and books.

Diploma

-Theology
-Leadership
-Missiology
N/B The diploma training program takes 1 year 1 day a week and charges are Kshs 1,500 per week inclusive of tea, lunch, notes and facilitation. have all the prerequisites for post secondary training in Theology
